65mm wide x 100mm high prestressed concrete lintel in a 1200mm length, designed for use in standard masonry openings. Manufactured from high-strength concrete and reinforced with prestressed steel wires, this lintel provides reliable structural support for brickwork above doors and windows.  


The textured finish improves mortar adhesion, ensuring secure bonding with surrounding masonry. Prestressing technology enhances load-bearing capacity while keeping section dimensions compact, making this lintel suitable for light to medium-duty applications.

Produced to consistent standards, the lintel offers durability against weathering and corrosion, ensuring long service life with minimal maintenance once installed

At 1200mm, the lintel is suited to typical domestic window and door spans. It provides a safe, economical, and durable solution for supporting masonry loads in residential and light commercial construction.

Features & Benefits

  • High-strength construction: Manufactured from dense, durable concrete reinforced with prestressed steel wires.
  • Improved bond: Textured finish enhances mortar adhesion for secure placement.
  • Durable performance: Resistant to fire, rot, and corrosion for long-term reliability.
  • Compact section: 65mm width and 100mm height suitable for brickwork with lighter loading requirements.
  • Standard application: 1200mm length suited to typical domestic window and door openings.

Applications

  • Window openings: Supports masonry above standard domestic window spans.
  • Door openings: Provides reliable support for brickwork over common door sizes.
  • Internal partitions: Suitable for non-load-bearing internal masonry where lintel support is required.
  • Light structural spans: Used where compact lintels are appropriate for lighter loads.

Compliance & Performance

  • Material: Dense concrete reinforced with prestressed steel wires.
  • Finish: Textured surface for mortar adhesion.
  • Load capacity: Designed for lighter masonry loads over standard openings.
  • Fire resistance: Non-combustible material, resistant to fire exposure.
  • Durability: Resistant to rot, corrosion, and long-term weathering.
  • Standards: Manufactured in accordance with relevant BS EN concrete lintel specifications.
  • Important note: Always verify load requirements against structural calculations and choose lintel size and section appropriate for the span and wall construction.
